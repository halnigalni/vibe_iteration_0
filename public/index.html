Saju (사주) — meaning "Four Pillars" — is Korea's ancient system of destiny reading. Unlike a psychic or tarot reading, Saju is a structured analytical framework built on thousands of years of Eastern philosophy. Think of it as a cosmic blueprint drawn from the exact moment you were born.

🏛️
3,000+ Years Old

Rooted in Chinese cosmology, refined through Korean culture for millennia. Korean royalty consulted Saju before every major decision.

📐
A System, Not a Guess

No crystal balls. Saju masters analyze your birth data using a precise framework of elements, stems, and branches — more like structured astrology than mysticism.

🔥
The Five Elements

Wood, Fire, Earth, Metal, and Water govern your personality and fate. Their balance — or imbalance — in your chart reveals your strengths and life patterns.

🐉
The 12 Animals

Each birth year carries one of 12 zodiac animals (Rat, Ox, Tiger…). Combined with your element, this shapes your temperament, relationships, and destiny arc.

Western Horoscope

Based on the position of the sun at birth. Changes by month. General personality traits.

vs
Korean Saju

Based on year, month, day and hour of birth. Far more specific — two people born on the same day can have very different readings.

✦  Today, over 70% of Koreans consult Saju at least once a year — not as superstition, but as a tool for self-understanding and timing life's big decisions. Even rational, educated Koreans keep going back. Now, for the first time, this tradition is available to you in English.
